About us!

In Ufer we connect with our users around the galaxy with the ways of transport that best suit their needs, there will always be a suitable option for each moment. Our goal is to transform cities and make intergalactic travel an affordable option for the maximum number of living creatures.

We bet on renewable energies, making the vast majority of our ships use an electric propulsion system. We are working to mobilize our entire fleet to clean energies, taking care of our galaxy is in the hands of all of us.
